Tagger Widget

Examples

Below are some examples of the tagger widget applied to a few select elements with varying options passed in. peopleData comes from the data.json file included for this demo.

Generic Multi-Select

  <script>
    $(function(){
      $('#sel1').tagger({
        availableTags: peopleData
      , preselectedTags: ["item0", "item1", "item8", "item11"]
      , baseURL: '../img/'
      , displayHierarchy: false
      , indentMultiplier: 2
      , fieldWidth: null
      });
    });
  </script>

Multi-Select with options

  <script>
    $(function(){
      $('#sel2').tagger({
        availableTags: peopleData
      , baseURL: '../img/'
      , placeholder: 'Person'
      , caseSensitive: true
      , displayHierarchy: true
      , indentMultiplier: 2
      , tabindexOffset: 100
      , noSuggestText: 'No People Match'
      , fieldWidth: '20em'
      , suggestMaxWidth: '70em'
      , sortedOutput: true
      });
    });
  <script>
